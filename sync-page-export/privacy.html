Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how TCGiant Sync Pro collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you purchase and use TCGiant Sync Pro, we may collect certain information, including:

  • Contact information (such as name and email address) provided during checkout.
  • License activation data, including the website URL where the plugin is activated, to verify license validity.
  • Support communications when you reach out for help.

Our payment processors (like Lemon Squeezy) handle your billing information securely. We do not store or process your sensitive payment details directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver the plugin files, license keys, and relevant purchase receipts.
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance.
  • Communicate important updates about the software.
  • Validate licenses and prevent unauthorized usage.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (like payment processors and email services) solely for the purpose of operating our business, under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Plugin Data and APIs

TCGiant Sync Pro connects your WordPress site directly to the eBay APIs. Your sensitive eBay authentication tokens are stored locally on your own WordPress server. Our servers do not intermediate, process, or store your actual inventory data or eBay credentials.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via hello@tcgiant.com
