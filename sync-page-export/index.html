Questions sellers usually ask

Use these as a starting point. You can swap these out later once you have customer feedback.

Who is this plugin for?

It is ideal for trading card sellers and other eBay sellers who want to mirror inventory into a WooCommerce store without manually updating products every time something sells.

Does it help prevent overselling?

Yes. The goal is to keep inventory updated between eBay and WooCommerce on an automatic sync cycle so the same item is less likely to remain live in both places after it sells.

How often does it sync?

The current page promises a 15-minute background sync interval. You can adjust this copy later if your production behavior changes.

Can I use this for a growing store?

Yes. The pricing and messaging are positioned for sellers who want a lightweight, affordable tool they can grow with instead of jumping straight into expensive marketplace software.
